CSIT6000N Group Project Daily Update

Project Information

This is the group project of CSIT 6000N Advanced Topics in Human-Computer Interaction. I worked with Shivani,Yizhang,Xinchen,Jingyi and Qianchen.The purpose of this project is human need in post pandemic era.

1. Background

The post-pandemic era has been marked by the devastating impact of the epidemic on the world, resulting in an overwhelming number of infections and fatalities. The crisis has compelled individuals to acknowledge that their unhealthy lifestyles could jeopardize not only their own well-being, but also that of those around them. As a result, there has been a growing awareness of the need to adopt healthier practices. Furthermore, the use of online medical services and health applications has witnessed a significant surge, indicating a shift in the way people perceive and manage their healthcare needs.

2. Emphasize

Our application serves three target type user which are elderly, people was inflected by Covid-19 and people who love fitness.

Insights:

Remind health problem

Record health indicators

Immediate health consultation

Drug purchasing

Needs:

Personalized health monitoring

Health alert when feeling uncomfortable

Timely help when sick

3. Ideate

We used a mind map in brainstrom stage.

During the discussion phase, we concluded some ways to improve health.
First, regular medical checkups can help people identify potential health problems and get timely treatment.
Secondly, regular and appropriate exercise helps to keep the body healthy and improve the immune system.
Good sleep is also essential in improving health, and people should try to avoid staying up late and overworking.
People can improve their health to some extent by adjusting their diet. For example, consume more vegetables and fruits.
In addition to physical health, there is also mental health. This can be achieved by doing something relaxing, such as yoga.
Finally, staying socially connected and staying in touch with family and friends can improve mental and emotional health.
Since mental health is more difficult to detect and adjust, we focus on physical health.

The functions of our application are mainly divided into two categories: health check and online health query.
For health checks, our application can collect and record users health indicators and use these data to remind them of some physical problems, so that they can better understand their health status. Also, our application can remind users to take medication on time. In addition, our application can record users daily exercise data and visualize these data on the device to help people develop their fitness plans.
For online health queries, our application provides users with online AI consultation and professional doctor consultation to ensure that the patients can receive timely treatment. Also, our application provides users with an online drug purchase function.

7.  Interview (Qualitative Analysis)

We asked a proxy user (Eddie) to evaluate our application who is 22 years old and have had covid-19 before.He worry he has a after effect of covid-19.Also,he love fitness

Interview questions in the following.

  1. Is it easy to navigate the app?
  2. Did you find all the features that were relevant to your needs, such as monitoring your physical health indicators and keeping track of your exercise routine?
  3. What do you think of the AI bots that can answer simple questions related to health?
  4. How helpful do you think the medication intake reminder and physical problem reminder features would be for you?
  5. How do you feel about the online drug purchase page? Do you think it is useful to have this feature integrated into the app?
  6. Do you have any suggestions or feedback on how we can improve the app to better suit your needs as a user?
  7. How likely are you to recommend this app to someone else who has had Covid-19 before and loves fitness?

The following framework is the organized data from interview .

Overall, Eddie had a positive experience with the app, finding it easy to navigate and appreciating the relevance and usefulness of its features. Their suggestions for improvement focused on providing more personalized experiences based on user health data. Eddie would highly recommend the app to others who have had Covid-19 and are interested in fitness. This feedback suggests that the app is well-designed to meet the needs of its target users and that incorporating personalized recommendations could further enhance its value.

8. Personal Reflections

I have never realized the importance of user case study and ideation before this project.In this project, I am responsible to construct a Hierarchical Task Analysis for clearly defining the goals, sub-goals and operations of the application. During this process, I continually consider what interaction design is most benefit to our target user.

When developing the prototype, I always reference the gestalt principles,10 heuristic usability to design the mobile user interface. After developed whole prototype,we prepare the interview question and invite the proxy user to evaluate our prototype.It is a special experience for me.

As a engineering student, I am familiar to do the Quantitative analysis in the lab report, or implementation project, research but without any experience for Qualitative analysis.This experience let me understand why need finding and evaluation is vital stage in the design period.Not doubt, questionnaire is fast and quantitative but collected data is more structured.The interview is more time consuming but it can gain the deeper understanding of the interviewees thoughts and also the answer is more open ended.I think it is the key point to make the design is more competitive.Because of the comment of interviewee, we can improve our application to meet the users need in the future.
